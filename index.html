About Me

I am an electrical engineering graduate from the University of Texas at Austin. I currently work along side a construction team in order to develop code compliant, cost efficient electrical system installation designs for Walker Engineering. After two years in the trade, I have come to realize how dependent we are on web based appilactions in our day to day to activites. Interaction with these applications, along with the lucrative opportunities in today's market, has sparked my interests in the tech industry. That sparked interest has led me to this course and needless to say, I am excited to begin my journey into learning the in's and out's of full stack developing.

While my ability to work with CSS/ Bootstrap is improving everyday, I am really looking forward to start delving into javascript since it is a little (actually alot) more intuitive than CSS. I know this is strictly an opinion, but CSS to me was more of a "let me see what doing this does to my web page" while javascript allows me to take a more logical approach to the problem at hand.
